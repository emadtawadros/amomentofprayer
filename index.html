Our Mission

We believe in the power of prayer. We believe that it's not just words or thoughts uttered in vein. Or that their sole purpose is just to make the praying person feel better about himself. No, we believe that prayers have actual tangible positive impact on both the lives of the praying and prayed for people. And collective prayer is one of the gratest, most noble and powerful things human nature is capable of. They can change lives and cause miracles. All great teachers throughout time have taught this. And we should listen. We believe in sending out love and good energy towards others. And that what goes around comes around. When you pray for someone else, not only you should feel good bercause you just made someone's life better, but you also just made your life better. Because the good you send out, comes back to you. And you will feel it.

We believe that praying is a universal phenomenon. It's not bound by religion, race, age, location. It's one of the greatest, most beautiful mysteries of human nature. And to be able to particpate in this unifying act is an amazing experience, it's humbling and it's a privlige.

As humans to humans, let's all pray for each other, and witness ourselves impacting each other's lives, through the power of prayer.
